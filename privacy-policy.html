Privacy Policy (ORBIT)

Last updated: May 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Deer Rock Studios ("Company", "we", "us"), as the publisher of ORBIT, collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the game.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account data: email address, user ID, and profile name (if you sign in with Google/Firebase).
  • Gameplay data: score, coins, game history, language, visual preferences, and progress.
  • Technical data: approximate country inferred from time zone, and push notification token (if you allow notifications).
  • Purchases: the App uses Stripe links for payments. We do not store card details on our servers.
  • Advertising: interstitial and rewarded ads may be shown in the App.

2. Guest Mode

If you play as a guest, some progress may be stored locally on your device and may be lost if browser data is cleared or you switch devices.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide sign-in and maintain your profile.
  • To save game progress, rankings, and stats.
  • To enable optional features such as push notifications.
  • To process purchases and improve game experience.
  • To prevent abuse and protect service security.

4. Third-Party Services

ORBIT may use these providers:

  • Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Messaging): account, data, and notifications.
  • Stripe: payments and subscriptions.
  • Google Ads / ad providers: in-game advertising.

These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Legal Basis and Consent

  • We process data necessary to run the App.
  • For push notifications, we request your explicit permission.
  • You can revoke permissions in your browser/device settings.

6. Data Retention

We retain data while your account is active or as needed to provide the service. You may request deletion of personal data.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of data.
  • Object to or limit certain processing.

8. Children

ORBIT is not intentionally directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country) without parental or guardian consent.

9. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will be published at this URL with an updated date.
