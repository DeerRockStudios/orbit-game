Privacy Policy (ORBIT)

Last updated: May 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Deer Rock Studios ("Company", "we", "us"), as publisher of ORBIT, collects, uses, and protects information when you use the game.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account data: email, user ID, profile name (if signing in via Google/Firebase).
  • Gameplay data: score, coins, game history, preferences, language, progress.
  • Technical data: approximate country from time zone, push token (if enabled).
  • Purchase data: payments via Stripe links. We do not store card details.
  • Ad-related events: interstitial and rewarded ad flow in-game.

2. How We Use Data

  • Operate core game features, login, rankings, and progression.
  • Provide support, improve quality, and prevent abuse.
  • Enable optional features such as push notifications.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Messaging).
  • Stripe (payments).
  • Ad providers (in-game ads).

4. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of personal data, subject to applicable law.
